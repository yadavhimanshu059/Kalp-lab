About Us

The Kanpur Language Processing (KaLP) Lab is a research group led by Himanshu Yadav in the Department of Cognitive Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India. We study language processing and language evolution in humans and machines. The goal is to understand how human language emerged, how it evolved, and how it works in the mind. See our research projects here.

Our Research

Our lab explores diverse areas including:

  • Human sentence comprehension
  • working memory
  • Individual differences
  • Language evolution
  • Interpretable AI
  • Cognitive modeling
  • Statistical methods in cognitive sciences

We use reading and eye-tracking experiments, Bayesian modeling, and large-scale language corpora analysis to study the above research areas.

Our Team

Our lab comprises PhD students, Research Assistants, Masters and undergraduate students, and independent collaborators.
